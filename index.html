About Us

Shenzhen Covien Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2022, is a photo booth manufacturer with in-house sheet metal production. We own laser cutting, bending, welding, and assembly lines. We hold design patents and support full customization. Each unit undergoes 100% power-on testing before shipment. We offer one-year warranty, remote support, and one-stop procurement for printers & cameras.

FAQs

Why should I choose Covien?

We are an integrated manufacturer and trading company, which directly reduces your procurement costs. We own our patents, ensuring stable and advanced products. With our own production line, you get reliable service.

the price is very low, but is the quality good?

We have our own sheet metal factory with years of experience. Key metal components like our iron photo booths are designed and produced in-house. From raw steel plate to final delivery, quality is controlled at every step visible and guaranteed.

Do you support customization?

Yes. We can customize color/appearance, cooling systems, speakers, screen size, laser logos, flight case handles, and more. We solve your real problems with one-stop purchasing.

What if the product gets damaged during use?

We offer remote technical support. For damage covered under warranty, replacement parts are provided free of charge.

If the goods are damaged during shipping, do I need to pay?

No. We recommend checking the packaging for damage upon opening. As long as the damage is not caused by the buyer, you will not bear any cost.
